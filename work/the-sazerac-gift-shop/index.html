The Sazerac Gift Shop Home Page

Sazerac is a New Orleans based alcoholic beverage manufacturer and importer. The company's portfolio ranges from extremely high end whiskies such as Pappy Van Winkle Bourbon and Glenfarclas Scotch to Rain, a line of organic vodkas, to popular shooter brands such as Fireball Whisky and Dr. McGillicuddy's Liqueur.

The Sazerac Gift Shop was created to sell merchandise for the products used to make the Sazerac Cocktail, the drink from which the company's name comes.

The site was built using a highly customized template on top of the ZenCart e-commerce platform. Products were broken down into 4 broad categories:

  1. Glassware & Bar Tools
  2. Bitters
  3. Clothing
  4. Gift Items
Category product list view

The look and feel of the site is deliberately "Retro" given the history of the drink. Many customization were made to the site including installation of the USPS module to pull in real-time shipping quotes based on weight, and integration of jQuery lightbox plugin for zooming in on product images.

jQuery lightbox plugin zooming in on Herbsaint Vintage Ad Posters product image

The site has become a focal point of the marketing activities for the brands featured, and has been expanded, building a separate portal for customers looking to buy wholesale quantities of bitters
