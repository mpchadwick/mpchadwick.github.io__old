Homepage of Fu-Ki Desktop Site

Fu-Ki is a premium alcohol brand imported from Japan with a line of products that includes a Junmai Sake, a Plum Wine, a Cherry Wine, and a Green Tea Wine. With an average annual growth rate of 4% in the Sake category since 2005, the brand needed an online presence to prove it's legitimacy.

My agency, CCM Marketing was awarded the project and, based on my 2 and a half years experience living in Japan, and knowledge of the culture I quickly became involved.

Working with the agency's chief creative director, we decided on three broad content categories for the site:

  1. About Fu-Ki: Product information such as serving sugestions, food pairings, tasting notes and ingredients
  2. Sake Education: Educational content such information about the Sake production process, history and varieties
  3. Drink Recipes: Drink recipes for each product

Using those broad categories as a base, key topics for the site were selected and a sitemap was developed.

Fu-Ki Sitemap

Next wireframes were developed for each visually distinct section of the site, and then high-fidelity mock-ups were created.

The site was built using a custom WordPress theme so that the client could easily update the content. In addition, mobile views for each page were created using jQuery mobile.

Mobile homepage and Fu-Ki Sake product page

The site was widely lauded by everyone at Fu-Ki and has become an invaluable asset to the brand.
